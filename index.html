About

TrainLink is an initiative organized by a group of Computer Science and Engineering students from FCT NOVA (NOVA School of Science and Technology), with the objective of learning and applying the main concepts of human-computer interaction and interface design.

Our application aims to remotely organize training in team sports, in order to facilitate the coach's work and maintain athletes informed about what will be done in training and important team information.

You can check the authors of this project, as well as the individual assignments of each one, and the different stages of the final project.
